# Keyboard-only Feature List

- ks_count: total key press events in the session
- ks_rate: keys pressed per second
- dwell_mean: mean time a key is held down (ms)
- dwell_std: std deviation of dwell time
- flight_mean: mean time between consecutive key presses (ms)
- flight_std: std deviation of flight time
- backspace_rate: ratio of backspace presses to total key presses
- digraph_mean: mean flight time for key pairs
- digraph_std: std deviation of digraph times
